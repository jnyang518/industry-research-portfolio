# Luckin Coffee Industry Research

## Overview

This repository contains a portfolio research report on China's freshly made coffee industry and Luckin Coffee.

The report studies the evolution of China's freshly made coffee market, Luckin Coffee's post-crisis operating recovery, store network expansion, financial quality, unit economics, valuation sensitivity, and key risk indicators.

This project is created for career portfolio demonstration purposes and does not constitute investment advice.

## Research Focus

The core question of this report is not simply whether Luckin Coffee has recovered after its financial crisis, but whether the company can continue to convert store density, digital user engagement, supply chain scale, and partnership expansion into stable store-level profit and cash flow.

## Key Questions

1. How much room remains for coffee consumption frequency growth in China?
2. Can Luckin's direct-operated and partnership store model support long-term capital efficiency?
3. Can rapid revenue growth continue to translate into operating profit and free cash flow?
4. How should investors evaluate Luckin after its post-crisis recovery?
5. What are the key risks from price competition, delivery cost, governance discount, and store saturation?
